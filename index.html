How to buy $BASETARD ?

Introduction

BASETARD is on Base The Layer 2 blockchain by Coinbase.
You’ll first have to integrate the Base Network into your wallet and then bridge over your ETH.
Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! We’ll walk you through the process with some easy-to-follow steps!

Step 1: Add the Base network on your wallet

Navigate to ‘Settings’ within your wallet and locate the networks section.
To add the network manually, input the required details for Base as follows:
– Network Name: Base
– RPC Endpoint: https://mainnet.base.org/
– Chain ID: 8453
– Currency Symbol: ETH
– Block Explorer: https://basescan.org
After saving, you can connect to Base simply by picking it from the network dropdown menu.

Step 2: Bridge to Base network

The next step is to deposit some ETH to Base. You can either:
– Transfer ETH to your Base wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance…. Remember to choose Base as the withdrawal network.
– Or use a bridge. Here are some bridge options:
Official Base Bridge: https://bridge.base.org/deposit
Orbiter: https://www.orbiter.finance/
Stargate: https://stargate.finance/
To use these bridges, connect your wallet, select the originating network you’re bridging from, set Base as the destination network, and enter the amount of ETH you wish to transfer.

Step 3: Initiate Token Acquisition

Change to the Base network by selecting it from the network dropdown menu in your wallet.
Visit SushiSwap: https://www.sushi.com/swap
Connect your wallet to the platform.
In the swap interface, choose a token in the bottom field and enter the official $BOGE token address:
0x00000000000000000000000000000000000

Step 4: Finalize Token Swap

Specify the amount of ETH you want to swap.
To adjust the slippage, click on the settings icon and set it to 3%.
Click on the ‘Swap’ button.
Approve and confirm the swap transaction in your wallet.

Congratulations, you’re now part of the BASETARD community! 🎉
